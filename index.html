Visual: stablecoin creation

An impact-backed stablecoin can be spun up without Maker-style collateral complexity—showing the essential path from collateral to impact.

1) Choose backing

Select impact tokens (carbon credits), ETH, or stables as reserves.

2) Mint stablecoin

Lock reserves and mint the stable asset; program basic ratios and ceilings only as needed.

3) Route impact

Direct minted liquidity into carbon vaults or community pools; track yield and CO₂ offsets.

4) Rebalance & prove

Periodic rebalances keep backing healthy; dashboards expose reserves, flows, and impacts.

Tokenizing impact projects

Focus on project data and credibility, not protocol jargon. Ownership stays durable; metadata stays updateable.

Carbon credit staking (ERC-4626)

A project spins up a vault; users deposit BCT and earn yield. Yield buys more credits, creating a deflationary green flywheel.

Fair-trade cacao batches (ERC-6960)

Core layer: proves ownership. Extension layer: GPS, harvest dates, certification PDFs—updated without reissuing the token.

Automated donations (ERC-995)

A Social Good Token routes a 1% tax per transfer to a verified charity address, sustaining funding without manual effort.

Impact flows

Practical, high-trust journeys for depositors, auditors, and treasury operators.

Carbon vault depositor

  • Deposit carbon credits → receive shares.
  • Vault deploys to DeFi, reinvests yield into more credits.
  • Dashboard shows PnL vs HODL and CO₂ impact.

RWA verifier

  • View ownership alongside live provenance fields.
  • Update GPS, harvest dates, or cert docs without changing ownership.
  • Timeline proves who changed what, with signatures and links.

DAO treasurer

  • Enable 1% tithing; set beneficiaries and settlement cadence.
  • Funds route automatically per transfer.
  • Impact dashboard shows monthly flows and receipts.
